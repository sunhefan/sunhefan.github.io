Also, I added my own sound that I recorded for my last project. Because there are chirp and water sounds, I decided to create a bird in my video art in order to make a connection. This video art project gives a sense of secret of a bird for viewers because the bird it came out from a picture on a wall because it is curious the outside world. Also, my video art is to express the curiosity because it is one of the important elements of our personalities.

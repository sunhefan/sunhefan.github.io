 Portfolio Artist Statement

Video Art

I recorded three different short video and I create a fake bird. The concept of the video art is a secret bird and to express the curiosity because it is one of the important elements of our personalities.I want to create a bird that flew out from the painting in the school's gallery and flied back into the painting when it is sunset.

Sound Art

There are three different sounds such as chirp, water, and beating the cup that I recorded from different places. The concept of the project is to create a natural environment of the sound.

Glitch

This glitch art is to corrupt data from our group image in order to create a visually corrupting effect. The original image was colored, it becomes gray because I use text editing software.
